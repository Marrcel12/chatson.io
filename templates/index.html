How does it Work?

Privacy is your choice
We believe that everyone has the right to anonymous correspondence. That is why we decided to create a system that will be easy to use, and at the same time will provide a fully private conversation. To which no one has access except people who know the room id and the encryption key
First protection
In the first step you have to choose or create/enter your own conversation room id. It is saved on our servers and encrypted. It allows another person to join the conversation.
You get assigned to one of the rooms
That's where we're gettin' started. After entering room_id, you will be joined to the room where in the top right corner you have to enter the message encryption key. This is key is not saved on the server. After sending the message, the server receives an already encrypted message by your key. Which is additionally encrypt with our key.
Invite your friend
To chat with another person completely anonymously, enter the same room id and then the same encryption code. Additionally, we recommend using a VPN and connecting from trusted networks to avoid other hacker attacks.
We keep your chats private
We do not collect any logs, about who connects to our server, and the encrypted chat that is stored with us on the server is worth nothing without user encryption keys.
You are safe
If a leak occurs or our server has been hijacked, your chats are securely encrypted and impossible to read without your key. The ciphers we use are approved and used by the NSA to protect top secret data. So if your encryption code is not hijacked from your computer or disclosed to an inappropriate person, you are completely safe.
